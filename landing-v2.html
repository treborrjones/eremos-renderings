The Concept

Five integrated environments. One membership.

The Eremos isn't a gym with add-ons. It's a complete performance ecosystem designed for people who train seriously and live deliberately. Every zone earns its square footage.

01

Train

10,000 SF of dedicated training zones. Rogue squat racks, Arsenal Strength plate-loaded machines, Eleiko competition platforms, a 40-yard turf lane, and conditioning equipment that belongs in an elite facility — not bolted to a TV.

02

Recover

1,500 SF recovery suite with cold plunge tubs (38-42°F), infrared sauna, Normatec compression, and percussion therapy. Glass-walled to the training floor — visible, but acoustically isolated.

03

Fuel

Full-service cafe and bar. Protein shakes, prepared meals, coffee. Craft beer, wine, and cocktails after 4 PM. A communal live-edge timber table where the post-workout conversation happens.

04

Work

1,200 SF coworking space with hot desks, monitors, and glass-walled meeting rooms. Deadlift 405 at noon. Take a Zoom call at 12:30. Acoustically separated. Fast Wi-Fi. No apologies.

05

Connect

1,800 SF social lounge with fireplace, leather club chairs, and digital community boards. League celebrations, milestone nights, and the kind of post-training gathering that doesn't happen in a parking lot.
